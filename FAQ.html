Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Absolutely! We offer a variety of vegetarian options:

  1. Caesar Salad with vegetarian-friendly ingredients
  2. Mukimo (mashed potatoes with green vegetables)
  3. Multiple vegetable-based snacks and side dishes
  4. Customizable menu options to suit dietary preferences

What are your opening hours?

Our restaurant is open with convenient hours:

  1. Open 7 days a week
  2. From 5:00 AM to 12:00 AM
  3. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner service available
  4. Late-night dining options

Do you offer reservations?

Reservation process is straightforward:

  1. Call our reservation desk directly
  2. Book online through our website
  3. Recommended for groups of 4 or more
  4. Advance booking helps ensure your preferred time

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment options:

  1. Cash
  2. Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard)
  3. Mobile payment solutions
  4. Contactless payments
